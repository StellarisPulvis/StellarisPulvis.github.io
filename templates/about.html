✨ 名字的由来

Stellaris Pulvis，拉丁语意为「星尘」。

这个名字来源于《空洞骑士》中信仰者神龛里的一句话——「星星与尘土」。初见时便被深深触动：星辰高远璀璨，尘土卑微平凡，但二者本是一体。我们都是星尘。

这个博客便是这样一粒星尘——微小，却承载着值得被分享的光芒。

🧑‍🚀 关于我

我是一个极简主义者和深度 DIY 爱好者。喜欢折腾自己的电脑，把系统一点点打磨成最符合自己心意的模样——极简、流畅、迅速。

对我而言，极简不是匮乏，而是去掉一切不必要的，留下最本质的。DIY 也不只是动手，更是一种创造和掌控的乐趣。

建这个博客，是为了分享想法、倾听声音，和大家一起成长。

✨ The Story Behind the Name

Stellaris Pulvis — Latin for «Stardust».

This name was inspired by a line from a shrine in Hollow Knight: «Stars and Dust». It struck me the moment I saw it — the stars shine distant and brilliant, while dust is humble and ordinary, yet they are one and the same. We are all made of stardust.

This blog is a speck of that dust — small, yet carrying light worth sharing.

🧑‍🚀 About Me

I'm a minimalist and a DIY enthusiast at heart. I love tinkering with my computer, shaping my system until it feels just right — minimal, smooth, fast.

To me, minimalism isn't about deprivation — it's about removing the unnecessary and keeping only what matters. DIY isn't just about making things; it's the joy of creating and being in control.

This blog exists to share ideas, listen to voices, and grow together.
